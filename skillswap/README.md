# SkillSwap Platform

A comprehensive peer-to-peer skill exchange platform built with Node.js, Express, MySQL, and vanilla JavaScript.

## Features

- **Skill Exchange**: Share and discover skills with video call scheduling
- **Course Sharing**: Upload and access educational courses
- **Notes & Resources**: Share study materials and notes
- **Project Collaboration**: Showcase and collaborate on projects
- **College Network**: Connect with students from your college
- **Dataset Sharing**: Share and access datasets for learning

## Tech Stack

- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js, MySQL
- **Frontend**: Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
- **Authentication**: JWT (JSON Web Tokens)
- **File Upload**: Multer
- **Password Hashing**: bcryptjs
